# Figures

This folder contains figures generated during the development of the swimming ring stability project.
### Figure 1: Model of one tilted swimming ring

Figure 1 illustrates the relative positions of the gravity center (GC) and buoyancy center (BC) in a tilted swimming ring.
### Animation 1: Buoyancy Center Shift and Stability Under Tilt

This interactive MATLAB simulation shows how the buoyancy center (BC) shifts as the swimming ring tilts from 0° to 90°. The relative position of the gravity center (GC) and buoyancy center (BC) provides a geometric basis for evaluating static stability and estimating the critical tilt angle.

This figure extends the original IA by treating the tilt angle as a variable rather than analyzing only a fixed tilted state.
